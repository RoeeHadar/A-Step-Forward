# A Step Forward — marketing copy

Single source of truth for every public surface (`apps/web/src/app/page.tsx`,
README hero, OG/Twitter cards, launch posts). Keep it warm, concrete, and short.

## One-liner (≤ 80 chars)

> An AI learning center that remembers you and grows with you.

## Two-liner

> An AI-native learning center with a roster of specialized agents — Tutor,
> Mentor, Coach, Reviewer — that teach, assess, remember, and evolve with each
> learner.

## Long-form value prop (≤ 280 chars, Twitter/X-friendly)

> A Step Forward is an AI learning center, not a chatbot.
> A team of agents tutors you, assesses you, remembers what you've learned,
> dreams on it overnight, and adapts. Open-source, multi-language, free to start.
> Try it → astepforward.app

## Hero (`apps/web/src/app/page.tsx`)

- **H1**: An AI learning center that remembers you.
- **H2**: A Tutor that asks, a Mentor that listens, a Coach that drills you.
  Memory that compounds.
- **Primary CTA**: Start learning — free
- **Secondary CTA**: How it works

## Three feature cards (used on landing + README)

1. **Agents, not chatbots.**
   A roster of specialists — Tutor (Socratic), Mentor (goals & habits), Coach
   (drills & FSRS), Reviewer (code & essays), Researcher, Note-Taker. Routed for
   you in milliseconds by an orchestrator.
2. **Memory that compounds.**
   Multi-layered, self-healing memory: episodic, semantic, procedural,
   affective. Nightly dreaming consolidates and decays. You can inspect, edit,
   or delete any memory.
3. **Citations from your own knowledge graph.**
   GraphRAG over a personal KG built from your lessons, notes, and uploads.
   Every claim links to the source.

## Differentiators (pick 3 for any given surface)

- **Open source, MIT.** Build on it, fork it, run it yourself.
- **Privacy-first.** PII redaction, encryption-at-rest, COPPA-aware child mode.
- **Multi-model.** Claude (primary), GPT (fallback), Gemini (multimodal). No
  vendor lock-in.
- **Eval-gated.** Every prompt, every agent, every change runs through
  `promptfoo` + `DeepEval` before it ships.
- **Multi-language.** English ships first, Hebrew RTL skeleton in the box,
  others to follow.

## Negative space — what we are NOT

- Not a chatbot.
- Not a homework-replacement generator.
- Not a content farm. Curriculum is curated and evaluated.
- Not a black box. Every answer is cited; every memory is inspectable.

## Voice

- Warm, direct, concrete. Skim-friendly.
- Talk to the learner ("you"), not about them.
- No marketing weasel words ("revolutionary", "best-in-class", "synergy").
- Cite specifics: "five agents", "nightly dreaming", "every claim cited".
