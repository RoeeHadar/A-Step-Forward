Hi Product Hunt 👋

I built A Step Forward because I wanted my own kids — and anyone else's — to
have something better than "talk to a chatbot" or "watch a video".

Inside the app you get a small team of agents, not one chatbot:

- **Tutor** — asks Socratic questions, adapts difficulty in real time.
- **Mentor** — goals, habits, motivation.
- **Coach** — drills, practice, FSRS-style spaced review.
- **Reviewer** — code, essays, problem solutions.
- **Researcher** — web + your own knowledge graph, every claim cited.

Two things make it special:

1. **Memory that compounds.** Episodic / semantic / procedural / affective
   memory, with a nightly "dreaming" job that consolidates and resolves
   contradictions. You can see every memory, edit it, or delete it.
2. **A personal knowledge graph.** Your lessons, notes, and uploads become a
   GraphRAG retrievable graph — every answer is grounded and cited.

It's open source (MIT), multi-language (English ships, Hebrew RTL in the box),
privacy-first (PII redaction, AES-GCM at rest, COPPA-aware child mode), and
eval-gated (`promptfoo` + `DeepEval`) so no untested prompt hits production.

Try it free at https://astepforward.app — and please tell me what's missing.
