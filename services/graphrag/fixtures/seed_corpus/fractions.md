# Fractions seed corpus

## Fractions

A **fraction** represents parts of a whole. The numerator counts selected parts; the denominator counts equal parts in one whole.

## Division

**Division** splits a quantity into equal groups. Division is a prerequisite for understanding fractions because fractions describe equal parts.

## Equivalent Fractions

Two fractions are **equivalent** when they name the same amount, such as `1/2` and `2/4`.

## Adding Fractions

To add fractions with the same denominator, add the numerators and keep the denominator.

## Common Misconception

Some learners believe larger denominators always mean larger fractions. This **misconception** opposes the concept of equivalent fractions.
