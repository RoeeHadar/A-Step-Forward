# Equivalent Fractions

**Equivalent fractions** name the same amount even though they look different. For example, **1/2**, **2/4**, and **3/6** all represent half of a whole.

## Multiply top and bottom by the same number

If you multiply the numerator and denominator by the same nonzero number, the value stays the same:

**1/2 = (1×2)/(2×2) = 2/4**

## Try it

Find two fractions equivalent to **3/5**. Show your work.

## Reasoning check

Why does multiplying both parts by 2 not change the amount? Explain using a picture or words.
