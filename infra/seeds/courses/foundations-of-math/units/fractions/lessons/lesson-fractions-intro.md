# Introduction to Fractions

A **fraction** represents parts of a whole. The top number (**numerator**) counts the parts you have; the bottom number (**denominator**) counts equal parts in the whole.

## Why fractions matter

Fractions appear everywhere: recipes, music, sports stats, and sharing pizza fairly with friends.

## Try it

If you cut a pizza into 4 equal slices and eat 1 slice, you ate **1/4** of the pizza. The numerator is 1 and the denominator is 4.

## Check your understanding

Draw or describe a situation where you would use the fraction **3/8**. Label the numerator and denominator.
