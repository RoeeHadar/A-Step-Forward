# Introduction to Percents

A **percent** is a ratio out of 100. **50%** means 50 out of 100, or half of a whole.

## Decimal link

Move the decimal point two places to convert:

- **0.25 → 25%**
- **75% → 0.75**

## Try it

Express **1/2**, **0.2**, and **40%** in all three forms (fraction, decimal, percent) where possible.
