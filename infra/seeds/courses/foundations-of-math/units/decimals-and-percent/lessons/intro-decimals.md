# Introduction to Decimals

A **decimal** uses a dot (decimal point) to show parts of a whole. In **0.75**, the 7 is in the tenths place (0.7) and the 5 is in the hundredths place (0.05).

## Fraction connection

**0.5** means five tenths, which is the same as **5/10** or **1/2**.

## Try it

Write 0.3 as a fraction in simplest form. Then compare 0.45 and 0.5 using < or >.
